Who is Speiser(DJ)?

Matthew Speiser, also known as Speiser(DJ),

is a electronic music audiophile, producer, and all genre DJ. He enjoys the purpose of music and from many different types of artists. His goals are to push the boundaries of sound design, format, and electronic music production. He wants to enlighten and inspire people through his music while providing a fun engaging experience or atmosphere. He describes his musical journey as a constant learning and discovery experience.

Originally he was trained instrumentally learning piano and guitar. Now after the huge electronic music scene expanding exponentially, he decided to venture into a more technologically creative avenue. His inspiration for music comes from a wide variety of genres. He understands the unique qualities of different genres and who they cater to is dynamic. Matthew Speiser has an extensive amount of business and real world experience. In todays music industry Matthew understands when trying to make a living as a musical artist, the quality of your art takes a backseat to the business practices.

“In order to be successful in the music industry, you need to be a successful business, in order to be a successful business, you need to have a great product”

Music is non-tangible, therefore you need to sell an experience. In todays musical culture you can find all sorts of music being followed by all sorts of people; some music that barely passes as music. However, being respectful to the subjective mind is very important. Speiser(DJ) has performed for an extensive variety of events with an all purpose catalog of music. These events include: Nightclubs, School Parties, Corporate Events, Radio Shows, Fashion Shows, Lounges, EDM events, Birthday Parties, Pole Dancing Competitions, and more.

“There are certainly different types of music for every mood or purpose, it’s about matching it perfectly”

Being a DJ is not easy because you have to adapt. Since music and the music industry are always changing, the DJ must change as well. Speiser(DJ) has a firm belief of supporting artists by purchasing music and production software legally. There is extensive amount of intentional piracy that is going unchecked and it is causing an unfair imbalance to both artists, and potential customers. This problem will not go away anytime soon, but it can be minimized.

Matthew Speiser greatly enjoys independent artists and wants to provide a culture of growth and social balance. He likes moderate, enthusiastic, and an engaging culture of people. He is not a fan of taking any sorts of illegal drugs with the exception of Cannabis. Matthew Speiser was diagnosed with Tourette Syndrome at age 10 and was on of prescription medications for most of his lifetime. Medications including: Chlonopin, Haldol, Chlonodine, Catapres, Abilify, Latuda, and others. After a lot of personal consideration he decided to stop using prescription medication all together and only use medical cannabis as needed. After 3 years of personal determination his symptoms and tics have greatly diminished. Despite his condition he was able to overcome and accomplish more than most would have projected.

He is ultimately a provider, a creator, and an aspiring inventor. Moving you to the music.

“To Create is To Believe”
